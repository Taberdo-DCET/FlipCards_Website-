How Usage Works

Your usage is based on the number of documents you successfully convert into flashcards each day. Here’s a detailed breakdown:

  • The Counter: This shows your remaining uses for the current 24-hour period (e.g., 8/10). The limit is determined by your account plan.
  • The Timer: This countdown shows exactly when your usage limit will reset. The reset happens automatically every day at midnight Coordinated Universal Time (UTC).
  • What Counts as a "Use": A "use" is only consumed when you upload a file and the AI successfully generates a set of flashcards from it. If an error occurs, your count will not be reduced.

Upgrading your plan is the best way to increase your daily generation limit.

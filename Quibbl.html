How to Play Quibbl

Game Objective

The goal is to guess the correct 'Term' based on the 'Definition' shown on the main screen. Type your answers in the chat box as quickly as you can!

For Players (Guests)

  • Wait for an invite from a host. Once you accept, you'll join their lobby.
  • Click the "I'm Ready" button to signal you're ready to start.
  • Once the game starts, watch the main screen for definitions and type your answers in the chat.

For Hosts

  1. Click 'Create Lobby' to start. This opens the lobby configuration menu.
  2. Click 'Select Set' to choose the flashcard deck for the game.
  3. Select the number of cards/rounds for the match.
  4. Invite players from the user list.
  5. Once all players are 'Ready', click 'Start' to begin the game!
